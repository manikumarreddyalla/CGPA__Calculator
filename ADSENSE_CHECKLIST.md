AdSense checklist for this CGPA Calculator
=========================================

This project is a static student utility. It can be submitted to AdSense after hosting, but approval is never guaranteed.

Before applying
---------------
- Host the `lite` folder on a real public URL.
- Prefer a custom domain instead of only a free subdomain.
- Replace `your-email@example.com` in `lite/contact.html`.
- Confirm these pages are reachable from the homepage:
  - `index.html`
  - `guide.html`
  - `faq.html`
  - `about.html`
  - `contact.html`
  - `privacy.html`
  - `terms.html`
- Keep adding useful student content, not only a calculator.

Safe ad placement for this site
-------------------------------
- Put ads only on normal content pages or calculator pages.
- Label ad areas only as `Advertisement` or `Sponsored Links`.
- Keep ads visually separate from navigation buttons, subject suggestions, grade selectors, export buttons, and remove buttons.
- Do not place ads inside pop-ups, floating boxes, emails, downloads, or fake menu areas.
- Do not make ads look like calculator controls.

Recommended first placements
----------------------------
- One display ad below the main calculator cards.
- One display ad near the end of `guide.html`.
- One display ad near the end of `faq.html`.

Avoid putting ads:
- In the top navigation.
- Between the subject search input and search suggestions.
- Next to the Export or Save Semester buttons.
- Beside the Remove button.
- In a way that shifts controls while a student is clicking.

Policy reminders
----------------
- Never click your own ads.
- Never ask friends, users, or classmates to click ads.
- Never write phrases like `click the ads`, `support us by clicking ads`, or `visit these ads`.
- Do not buy paid-to-click, autosurf, click-exchange, bot, or fake traffic.
- Do not promote the site through spam emails or unwanted third-party ads.
- Do not use pop-ups, forced redirects, malware, or automatic downloads.
- Do not frame someone else's website without permission.
- Do not use Google logos or wording that makes the site look like an official Google or Vel Tech service.

When AdSense gives publisher details
------------------------------------
- Add the real AdSense script exactly as Google provides it.
- If Google asks for `ads.txt`, create it with the exact publisher ID from AdSense.
- Do not create a fake `ads.txt` before you have the real publisher ID.
