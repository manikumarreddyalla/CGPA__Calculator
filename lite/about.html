Purpose

Vel Tech CGPA Calculator helps students quickly estimate semester grade point average and cumulative grade point average from subject credits and grades. It is designed for personal planning, academic awareness, and checking approximate outcomes.

Independence

This website is not an official Vel Tech service, transcript system, exam portal, or university result page. The name is used only to describe the student audience and subject list context.

Content and Accuracy

The subject list and grade scale are provided as a helpful reference. Academic rules may change by regulation, department, and batch, so students should confirm final credits, grades, and CGPA with official university records.

How Results Are Calculated

The calculator multiplies each subject credit by the selected grade point, adds the weighted grade points, and divides by total credits. Saved semesters are combined using the same weighted method to estimate CGPA.
