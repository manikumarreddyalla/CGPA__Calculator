Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about results, storage, and subject data.

Calculator Guide About Contact Privacy Terms

Is this result official?

No. The result is only an estimate based on the subjects, credits, and grades entered in this browser. Always verify official results with your university portal, exam cell, or academic department.

Where is my data stored?

Saved semesters are stored in your browser using localStorage. The calculator does not send your grades to a server.

Can I calculate CGPA for multiple semesters?

Yes. Add subjects for one semester, save it, and then repeat the same process for the next semester. The saved semester list is used to estimate CGPA.

What should I do if a subject is missing?

Use the contact page to report the subject code, subject name, department, regulation, and credits. The subject list can be updated in a future version.

Why does RA count as zero?

RA is treated as 0 grade points in this calculator because it indicates a failed or arrear result. Use your official university rulebook for final academic decisions.
