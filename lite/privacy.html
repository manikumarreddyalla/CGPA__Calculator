Vel Tech CGPA Calculator is a browser-based academic utility for estimating SGPA and CGPA. This policy explains what information may be stored or processed when you use the website.

Information You Enter

The grades and saved semester data you enter are stored locally in your browser using localStorage. This site does not run a backend database and does not upload your grades to a server.

Local Storage

The site uses localStorage to remember saved semesters on the same browser and device. You can remove this data by using the Clear All button in the calculator or by clearing your browser site data.

Cookies and Advertising

If advertising is enabled in the future, third-party vendors, including Google, may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website or other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables Google and its partners to serve ads based on visits to sites on the internet.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings. You may also opt out of some third-party vendors' use of cookies for personalized advertising at aboutads.info.

Analytics

This version does not include analytics tracking code. If analytics is added later, this policy should be updated to explain what is collected and why.

Children's Privacy

This calculator is intended for college-level academic use. It does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Contact

For privacy questions or correction requests, use the contact page.

This privacy policy is provided for transparency and should be reviewed before publishing the site with ads.
